Our Story

From passion to plate - the journey of Odin Recipes

The Beginning

Odin Recipes was born in August 2022 from a simple desire to share the rich, vibrant flavors of Nigerian cuisine with the world. What started as a learning project for a passionate developer has grown into a comprehensive resource for authentic Nigerian recipes.

I believe that food is more than just sustenance - it's a bridge between cultures, a keeper of memories, and a source of joy that brings families together. Every recipe on this site carries the warmth of Nigerian hospitality and the wisdom of generations who perfected these dishes.

"Cooking is about passion, so it may look slightly temperamental in a way that it's too assertive to the naked eye."
- My cooking philosophy

Meet Whitefire

Developer & Food Enthusiast

Hi, I'm Whitefire! I've been a frontend developer since August 2022, and Odin Recipes represents my first major project where I combined my passion for coding with my love for Nigerian cuisine.

This website started as a simple HTML/CSS project but evolved into a full-featured recipe platform with interactive elements, responsive design, and modern web technologies. Every line of code was written with love and every recipe was carefully researched and tested.
